 C O M M A N D S :

----------------------------------------------------------
 :n! Create a new note.

 I N S T R U C T I O N S :

 1.-Define a "keyword" to identify your 
    data categories.
 2.-Write your Note related to the "keyword" category you
    have defined before.
 3.-Enjoy your Notes managed by "keywords".
